## 脚本一
```shell
grep ":3306.*ESTABLISHED" nowcoder.txt | awk '{print $5}' | cut -d ":" -f 1 | sort | uniq -c | sort -nr | sed 's/[ \t]*//'
```


## 脚本二
```shell
awk '/:3306.*ESTABLISHED/{map[$5]++} END{for(k in map) print map[k],k}' nowcoder.txt | sed 's/:3306//' | sort -nr
```
